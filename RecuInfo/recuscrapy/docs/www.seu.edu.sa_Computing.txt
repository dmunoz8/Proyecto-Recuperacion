          <h1>
           Dean's Word
          </h1>
          <div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl01_label" style="display:none">
           Page Image
          </div>
          <div aria-labelledby="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl01_label" class="ms-rtestate-field" id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl01__ControlWrapper_RichImageField" style="display:inline">
           <div class="ms-rtestate-field">
           </div>
          </div>
          <br/>
          <p class="news_details">
          </p>
          <div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl02_label" style="display:none">
           Page Content
          </div>
          <div aria-labelledby="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl02_label" class="ms-rtestate-field" id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_DeviceChannelPanel2_ctl02__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" style="display:inline">
           <p>
           </p>
           <div>
            In the name of God and His blessing be upon our Prophet Muhammad and all his family and companions;
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            With the advancement, expansion and application of computer technology, came an ever increasing demand for specialized workforce. This has necessitated supporting computer colleges in order to prepare larger numbers of graduates to meet the requirements of manpower development and technical workforce needed for spreading the use of IT.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia has been among the countries which have invested in preparing local experts for the development of Saudi citizens who are the main goal in development plans.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            Driven by the above purposes, the CCI had to design an ambitious academic plan based on sound scientific principles, supported by a team of academic and technical experts. In doing this we have endeavored to tap pedagogical and technical expertise and local and international standards through cooperation agreements with a number of international universities and institutes.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            Through its mission, CCI will work towards building a student body that is able and ambitious, since our mission which teaches students and helps building our country. In this direction we will spare no effort in promoting the CCI to the rank of pioneering college in the KSA, utilizing continuous up-grading of curricula which meet the need of labor market and the economy of KSA.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            We are assisted by a bright team of faculty and MA staff, which will be expanded and improved by recruiting distinguished academics in the field from various nationalities. We hope that our college will be able to combine academic, administrative and technical standards and quality.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            Finally, I would like to call on students to nourish in themselves a sense of responsibility towards their studies and to produce the best in them for this country led by the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Abdullah bin Abdulaziz, the initiator of SEU and the pioneering supporter of educational revival of KSA.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div style="text-align:justify;">
            We welcome suggestions that help improve our college and we encourage continuous dialogue though telephone, e-mail and other media.
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
            We ask Allah to help us in our endeavor.
           </div>
           <div>

             Dean of CCI
