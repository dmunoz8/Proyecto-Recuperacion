             <header>
              <h1 class="node-title">
               School of Computing &amp; Informatics
              </h1>
             </header>
             <div class="content clearfix">
              <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
               <div class="field-items">
                <div class="field-item even">
                 <p>
                  In the School of Computing and Informatics, we help our students develop skills in programing, communication, mathematics, algorithmic reasoning and the underlying principles of computing. Our goal is to equip students with the ability to adapt to and become important developers of these fast-paced and rapidly changing fields.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  We offer six degree programs among three academic units: the
                  <a href="http://www.cacs.louisiana.edu">
                   Center for Advanced Computer Studies
                  </a>
                  ,
                  <a href="/node/62">
                   computer science program
                  </a>
                  , and
                  <a href="/node/9">
                   informatics program
                  </a>
                  . You can choose from a diverse set of courses every semester and have access to state-of-the-art facilities in James R. Oliver Hall, the newest academic building on UL Lafayette’s campus.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Our school has strong relationships with local and national technology companies that actively seek out our graduates for internships and careers. Learn how you can become a part of our school and start building your future today!
                 </p>
