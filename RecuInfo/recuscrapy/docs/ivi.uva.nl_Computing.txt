           <h2>
            ACM-W Europe Celebration of Women in Computing
           </h2>
           <div class="show-on-print">
            <span class="agenda-date english">
             <strong>
              <span class="agenda-day">
               12
              </span>
              Sept
              <span class="agenda-year">

            </span>
           </div>
           <p class="meta">
            Event
           </p>
           <p class="intro">
            The Association for Computing Machinery-Women supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women.
           </p>
           <p>
            ACM organizes the 3rd ACM-W Europe Celebration of Women in Computing to be held in Linz, Austria. This is the third edition of the annual womENcourage event aimed at networking and exploring career opportunities for women in computer science and related disciplines. Consider participating and/or spreading the news to all who might be interested.
           </p>
           <h3>
            More information
           </h3>
           <p>
            For more information please read the website ACM-W.
           </p>
           <div class="clearfix">
            <a class="call-to-action fluid-width" href="http://women.acm.org/" target="_self">
