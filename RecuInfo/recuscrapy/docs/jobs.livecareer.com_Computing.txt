            <h3>
             Job Description
            </h3>
            Drexel University
            <br/>
            College of Computing &amp; Informatics
            <br/>
            Faculty Positions in Computer Science &amp; Data Sciece
            <br/>
            The College of Computing and Informatics at Drexel University invites applications for for open rank tenure-track faculty positions in both the Information Science and Computer Science departments. Candidates should have a Ph.D. at the time of appointment and a record of high-quality scholarly activities. The College is home to a vibrant research culture in areas as diverse as algorithms and theory, artificial intelligence, computer vision, databases, distributed systems, geographic information science, human-centered computing, information policy, machine learning, privacy and security, programming languages, social-media analysis, and visual analytics. Successful applicants will be expected to teach at the undergraduate and graduate levels, establish strong sponsored research programs, advise undergraduate and graduate students, and be involved in service to the college, the university, as well as the global academic community.
            <br/>
            Drexel is a private university committed to research with real-world applications. The university has over 25,000 students in 14 colleges and schools and offers about 200 degree programs. The College of Computing and Informatics has about 75 faculty and 2,300 students. Drexel has one of the largest and best known cooperative education programs in the country, with over 1,200 co-op employers. Drexel is located on Philadelphias Avenue of Technology in the University City District and at the hub of the academic, cultural, and historical resources of the nations fifth largest metropolitan region.
            <br/>
            Evaluation of applications will be conducted on a rolling basis with applications accepted until January 1, 2016. Successful applicants must demonstrate potential for research and teaching excellence in the environment of a major research university. To be considered, please apply for one of our open positions at
            <br/>
            Your application should consist of a cover letter, CV, and brief statements describing your research program and teaching interests. Letters of reference will be requested from candidates who are invited for a campus interview. Electronic submissions in PDF format are required.
            <br/>
            Drexel University is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer. The College of Computing &amp; Informatics is especially interested in qualified candidates who can contribute to the diversity and excellence of the academic community. Background investigations are required for all new hires as a condition of employment, after the job offer is made. Employment will be contingent upon the University's acceptance of the results of the background investigation.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The Chronicle of Higher Education. Category: Education, Training &amp; Library, Keywords: Information Science - Faculty Position
