       <h2 class="title" id="page-title">
        Early Stage Development of Technologies in Biomedical Computing, Informatics, and Big Data Science (R43/R44)
       </h2>
       <div class="tabs">
       </div>
       <div id="content" role="main">
        <div class="region region-content">
         <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
          <div class="content">
           <div about="/research/fo/early-stage-development-technologies-biomedical-computing-informatics-and-big-data-6" class="node node-funding-opportunities node-promoted clearfix" id="node-23096" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
            <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Early Stage Development of Technologies in Biomedical Computing, Informatics, and Big Data Science (R43/R44)" property="dc:title">
            </span>
            <div class="content">
             <div class="field field-name-field-link field-type-link-field field-label-hidden">
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even">
                <a href="http://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/pa-files/PA-14-154.html">
                 Full announcement
                </a>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="field field-name-field-number field-type-text field-label-inline clearfix">
              <div class="field-label">
               Number:
              </div>
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even">
                PA-14-154
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="field field-name-field-due-date field-type-datetime field-label-inline clearfix">
              <div class="field-label">
               Due Date:
              </div>
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even">
                <span class="date-display-single" content="2016-04-05T07:00:00-07:00" datatype="xsd:dateTime" property="dc:date">
                 April 5, 2016
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                <p>
                 This funding opportunity announcement (FOA) encourages Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) grant applications from small business concerns (SBCs) that propose development of a broad base of innovative technologies in biomedical computing, informatics, and Big Data Science that will support rapid progress in areas of scientific opportunity in biomedical research. It is expected that this research and development is conducted in the context of important biomedical and behavioral research problems. As such, applications are intended to develop enabling technologies that could apply to the interests of most NIH Institutes and Centers and range from basic biomedicine to research in all relevant organ systems and diseases. Major themes of research include collaborative environments; data integration; analysis and modeling methodologies; and novel computer science and statistical approaches.  New opportunities are also emerging as large and complex data sets are becoming increasingly available to the research community.
                </p>
