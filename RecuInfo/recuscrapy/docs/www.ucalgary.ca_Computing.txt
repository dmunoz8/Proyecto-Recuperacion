      <h1 class="access">
       ICIC Home
      </h1>
      <div class="region region-content">
       <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
        <div class="content">
         <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
          <div class="field-items">
           <div class="field-item even">
            <p style="text-align: center; color: #0000ff; font-size: 28px; font-weight: bold; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 0px;">
             The International Institute of Cognitive Informatics and Cognitive Computing (
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              I
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
              CI
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              C
             </span>
             )
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 0px;">
             <img height="200" src="/icci_cc/files/icci_cc/images/icic_logo.png" width="261"/>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 18px; margin-top: 8px; margin-bottom: 0px;">
             <a href="/icic" style="color: #0000ff;">
              http://www.ucalgary.ca/icic
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14px; font-weight: bold;">
             <img height="11" src="/icci_cc/files/icci_cc/images/list_icon01.jpg" width="11"/>
             Mission of
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              I
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
              CI
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              C
             </span>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 14px; margin-right: 30px; font-family: Times New Roman;">
             The International Institute of Cognitive Informatics and Cognitive Computing (
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              I
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
              CI
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              C
             </span>
             ) is an international not-for-profit association of researchers and practitioners in a wide range of contemporary disciplines such as cognitive informatics, abstract intelligence, cognitive computing, denotational mathematics, cognitive computers, computational intelligence, cognitive systems, cognitive robots, cognitive Internet, cognitive linguistics, cognitive learning engines, cognitive inference engines, brain informatics, neuroinformatics and software science.
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              I
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
              CI
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              C
             </span>
             focuses not only on basic theories and denotational mathematics underpinning the aforementioned scientific and engineering disciplines, but also on their applications in industries, health care, societies and humanities.
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 14px; margin-right: 30px; font-family: Times New Roman;">
             The mission of
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              I
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
              CI
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: Euclid Math Two;">
              C
             </span>
             is to promote collaborative research in cognitive informatics and cognitive computing, to establish a repository of the latest research results, to develop joint funding opportunities, to train and exchange graduate students, and to strengthen the applications of cognitive informatics and cognitive computing in industries.
            </p>
            <p style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14px; font-weight: bold;">
             <img height="11" src="/icci_cc/files/icci_cc/images/list_icon01.jpg" width="11"/>

            </p>
