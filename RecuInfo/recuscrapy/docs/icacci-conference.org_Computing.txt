        <div class="title">
         <h2 class="title" id="page-title">
          Welcome to WCI 2015!
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="region region-content">
         <div class="block block-system" id="block-system-main">
          <div class="content">
           <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="Welcome to WCI 2015!" property="dc:title">
           </span>
           <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="0" datatype="xsd:integer" property="sioc:num_replies">
           </span>
           <div class="content">
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p class="rtecenter">
                <a href="http://dl.acm.org/citation.cfm?id=2791405" target="_blank">
                 <span style="font-size:16px">
                  <strong>
                   Proceedings of WCI-2015 are now available online in the ACM Digital Library
                  </strong>
                 </span>
                </a>
                <span style="font-size:16px">
                </span>
               </p>
               <p class="rtejustify">
                Women represent approximately 51% of the total population. Over the course of history, the demographics of the workforce have changed significantly. Women continue to advance, holding important roles and positions in both academia and industry. Although progress has been made, the precipitous journey is not yet complete. Global concerns about current and future roles of women in computing occupations gained more importance with the emerging information age.
               </p>
               <p class="rtejustify">
                The
                <span style="color:rgb(115, 0, 171); font-size:12.7272720336914px">
                 <strong>
                  Third International Symposium on Women in Computing and Informatics (WCI-2015)
