           Here I am providing the paper pattern of AMIE for your idea.
           <br/>
           Paper Pattern for AMIE is given below:
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Each paper is 3 hours long The maximum number of papers a candidate can appear at a time is:
           <br/>
            Section A : 4 papers
           <br/>
            Section B : 4 papers
           <br/>
           Section- A Examination:
           <br/>
           The examinations are held twice every year.
           <br/>
           Subjects in Section - A :
           <br/>
           Diploma Stream:
           <br/>
           Fundamentals of Design and Manufacturing
           <br/>
           Society and Environment
           <br/>
           Material Science and Engineering
           <br/>
           Computing and Informatics
           <br/>
           Non-Diploma Stream:
           <br/>
           Fundamentals of Design &amp; Manufacturing
           <br/>
           Engineering Mathematics
           <br/>
           Material Science and Engineering
           <br/>
           Electronics &amp; Instrumentation
           <br/>
           Computing and Informatics
           <br/>
           Society and Environment
           <br/>
           Engineering Drawing and Graphics
           <br/>
           Electrical Science
           <br/>
           Mechanical Science
           <br/>
           Engineering Physics And Chemistry
           <br/>
           <br/>
           After Section A, the student will be admitted to appear for the Section B examination
           <br/>
           This consists of 9 papers of which 6 are compulsory and 3 optional.
           <br/>
           Following engineering branches are covered:
           <br/>
            Aerospace Engineering
           <br/>
            Electronics and Communications Engineering
           <br/>
            Mechanical Engineering
           <br/>
            Metallurgical and Material Science Engineering
           <br/>
            Mining Engineering
           <br/>
            Production Engineering
           <br/>
            Architecture Engineering
           <br/>
            Chemical Engineering
           <br/>
            Civil Engineering
           <br/>
            Computer Science &amp; Engineering
           <br/>
            Electrical Engineering
           <br/>
            Textile Engineering
           <br/>
           Minimum time limit to complete Section A:
           <br/>
           Diploma stream: 6 months
           <br/>
           Non-Diploma stream: 1.5 years
           <br/>
           Minimum time limit to complete Section B:
           <br/>
           Diploma and Non-Diploma stream: 1.50 years
           <br/>
           <br/>

            AMIE Question Paper
